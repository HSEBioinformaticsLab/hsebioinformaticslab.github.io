  • Comparative Genomic Analysis of the Regulation of Aromatic Metabolism in Betaproteobacteria

    Authors: I. Suvorova, M. S. Gelfand

    Journal: Frontiers in Microbiology, 2019, Vol. 10, pp. 1-18

    Added: October 31, 2019

    Abstract: Aromatic compounds are a common carbon and energy source for many microorganisms, some of which can even degrade toxic chloroaromatic xenobiotics. This comparative study of aromatic metabolism in 32 Betaproteobacteria species describes the links between several transcription factors (TFs) that control benzoate (BenR, BenM, BoxR, BzdR), catechol (CatR, CatM, BenM), chlorocatechol (ClcR), methylcatechol (MmlR), 2,4-dichlorophenoxyacetate ...
